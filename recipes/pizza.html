Description

A simple cheese pizza is the epitome of comfort food, featuring a golden, crisp crust that serves as the perfect foundation for a rich tomato sauce. This sauce is spread evenly over the dough, imparting a tangy and slightly sweet flavor. The star of the dish, however, is the generous layer of melted mozzarella cheese, which bubbles and browns to perfection in the oven. Each bite delivers a satisfying combination of crunchy crust, savory tomato sauce, and gooey, stretchy cheese, making it a beloved classic that's both uncomplicated and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Shredded Cheese
  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Lay the pizza dough on a flat surface
  3. Add tomato sauce (amount is what you perfer )
  4. Add shredded cheese
  5. Place in oven and leave it in for 15-20 minutes or till golden crust and melted cheese
