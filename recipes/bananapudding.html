Description

Banana pudding is a delightful and nostalgic dessert that layers creamy goodness with sweet, fruity flavors. This classic treat starts with ripe, sliced bananas that are evenly distributed in a dish, creating a fresh and naturally sweet base. Over the bananas, a rich, velvety vanilla pudding is poured, its smooth texture perfectly complementing the soft fruit. To add a bit of crunch and depth, layers of crisp vanilla wafers are interspersed throughout, which soften slightly as they absorb the pudding's flavors. Finally, a generous topping of whipped cream or meringue is spread over the top, adding a light and airy finish to each spoonful. Served chilled, banana pudding is a perfect blend of textures and flavors, making it a favorite for gatherings and a comforting indulgence for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milke
  • 1 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 3 cups cold skim milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • Â½ (12 ounce) package vanilla wafers
  • 4 bananas, sliced

Steps

  1. Gather ingrediants
  2. Beat cream cheese in a large bowl until fluffy. Beat in condensed milk, then pudding mix. Gradually mix in cold milk until smooth, followed by vanilla
  3. Fold in 1/2 of the whipped topping.
  4. Line the bottom of a 9x13-inch dish with vanilla wafers.
  5. Arrange sliced bananas evenly on top.
  6. Cover with pudding mixture
  7. Top with remaining whipped topping.
